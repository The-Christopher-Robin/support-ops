# New customer welcome checklist

Send to every new customer on day 1 of their subscription so they have a
clear path to first-value.

## Day 1
- Accept the admin invite and enroll in MFA.
- Invite the rest of their team (up to the seat count on their plan).
- Import initial data (see data-import.md).

## Day 3
- Connect their first integration (payroll provider, accounting software).
- Review notification preferences so the right people get alerts.

## Day 7
- Schedule the 30-minute onboarding call with their customer success rep.
- Review their first report and confirm the numbers line up with their
  source system.
