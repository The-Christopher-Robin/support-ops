# Refund policy

We issue refunds within 30 days of the original invoice charge. To request one
ask the customer to share the invoice number, the last four digits of the card
used, and a brief reason. Anything older than 30 days needs manager approval.

## Common cases
- Duplicate charge: refund the duplicate in full, no approval needed.
- Cancellation inside the 30-day window: prorate to the cancel date.
- Cancellation after 30 days: offer account credit instead of cash refund.
