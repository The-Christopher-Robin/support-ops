# Plan upgrades and downgrades

Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated from the upgrade date.
Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle so customers do
not lose access to paid features mid-cycle.

## Edge cases
- If the customer has add-on seats, downgrading the base plan automatically
  drops the seat count to the new plan's maximum. Always call this out.
- Annual plans cannot be downgraded mid-term. Offer a credit for the unused
  portion if the customer is moving to a cheaper plan on renewal.
