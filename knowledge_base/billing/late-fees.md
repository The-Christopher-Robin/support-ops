# Late fees

Late fees are 1.5% of the outstanding balance and accrue monthly. For a
first-time late payment we will waive the fee if the customer pays the full
invoice within 10 days of the original due date.

## What to do
- Confirm the account has no prior late-fee waivers in the last 12 months.
- If clear, apply a one-time waiver with reason code FIRST_TIME.
- If there is a prior waiver, point the customer at payment plans before
  escalating to the collections queue.
