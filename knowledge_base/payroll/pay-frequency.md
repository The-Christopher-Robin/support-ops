# Pay frequency changes

Pay frequency changes (weekly to biweekly, biweekly to semi-monthly, etc.)
require at least one full pay cycle of notice so the prenote and timesheet
rollover process cleanly.

## Process
1. Customer admin submits the change in Settings > Payroll > Schedule.
2. Our team validates no open off-cycle runs exist.
3. The first pay under the new frequency includes a reconciliation line so
   employees can see the prior and new schedule on one stub.
