# Direct deposit setup

Direct deposit enrollment requires a voided check or the ABA routing number
plus the account number. Once submitted, it takes two pay cycles for the
bank verification to complete. During that window the employee is paid by
paper check to the address on file.

## Common issues
- Routing number mistyped: the bank rejects the prenote, employee is paid by
  paper check until they resubmit.
- Closed account: same as above, employee should update through the portal.
- Split deposit across two accounts: allowed, but each account counts as its
  own prenote cycle.
