# W-2 and tax forms

W-2 forms are issued by January 31 each year. They are available in the
employee portal under Documents > Tax Forms. Employees who opted out of
paperless delivery receive a paper copy by mail in the first week of February.

## Reissue requests
- If an employee lost their W-2, they can download a copy from the portal at
  any time. No reissue fee.
- For a corrected W-2 (W-2c), confirm the error in writing, then submit a
  correction request to payroll-ops with the original W-2 and the correction.
- Prior-year W-2s are available for 7 years in the portal.
