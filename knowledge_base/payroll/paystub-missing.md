# Missing or wrong paystub

If an employee reports a missing paystub, check the pay calendar first.
Paystubs publish the morning of payday, not the day before.

## Diagnostic steps
1. Confirm the pay-period end date and the pay date.
2. Check whether the employee has any leave codes on that cycle that could
   zero out the stub (unpaid leave, sabbatical).
3. Verify that the employee's employment status was active on the pay date.
4. If all three check out, escalate to payroll-ops with the employee ID.
