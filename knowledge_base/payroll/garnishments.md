# Wage garnishments

Garnishments are set up by payroll-ops after receiving a valid court order.
Employees cannot modify an active garnishment directly; changes require an
updated order from the issuing authority.

## Employee questions
- Amount withheld per cycle: visible on the paystub under Deductions.
- Duration: visible on the original court order; ask the employee to pull
  their copy.
- Hardship reductions: require the employee to file with the issuing court.
  We cannot reduce the amount without an updated order.
